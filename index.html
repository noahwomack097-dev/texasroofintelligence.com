Why it matters

Most homeowners have no idea what's happened to their roof.

Resale value

Undocumented roof damage is one of the first things a buyer's inspector finds — and one of the most common reasons deals fall apart at closing.

A written inspection report documenting legitimate depreciation and deferred maintenance can support your Collin County appraisal protest.
